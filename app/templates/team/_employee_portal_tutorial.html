Step 1 of 7

Use the left menu to move around.

Dashboard is your home base. Hours, Announcements, Schedule, Time off, Policies, Supply, Profile, and Ops tools all live in this left menu.

Step 2 of 7

Start with today's shift.

This top card shows whether you work today, whether Clockify says you are clocked in, how many hours you have today, and break status.

Step 3 of 7

Check what needs attention.

The “What do I need to do today?” card tells you if there are announcements, unsigned policies, clock status issues, or anything else to handle.

Step 4 of 7

Use Hours for time and pay.

Hours this week and Estimated pay use Clockify hours and your pay rate, not the schedule. If pay looks wrong, open Hours and tap Ask for help.

Step 5 of 7

Read updates and upcoming shifts.

Announcements show team updates. Upcoming shows the next shifts posted for you. Use Open schedule when you want the full calendar.

Step 6 of 7

Finish your profile checklist.

Add your phone number, emergency contact, sign policies, and connect Clockify so managers can reach you and your hours/pay can calculate correctly.

Step 7 of 7

Ask for help from any page.

Use Ask for help whenever something is confusing, wrong, or missing. It helps your manager know which page you were on and what you were trying to do.
