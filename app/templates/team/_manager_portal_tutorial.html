Fast path to schedule someone
  1. Open Admin > Schedule.
  2. Tap Edit schedule.
  3. Add the person, click the day, set the time, then save.
Step 1 of 7

Use the left menu when you are lost.

If your job is scheduling, go to Admin > Schedule. Dashboard, Hours, Announcements, Notifications, Schedule, Time off, Policies, Supply, Profile, and Ops tools stay in this left menu.

Step 2 of 7

Check today's status first.

The top card shows your shift, clock status, hours today, and break status. Managers still use the shop iPad for clocking in and out.

Step 3 of 7

Clear anything marked important.

The attention card and profile checklist show anything blocking your own portal setup, policies, Clockify connection, or unread team updates.

Step 4 of 7

Build shifts from Admin.

Team schedule, Supply queue, Time off queue, and Announcements admin are the manager tools for running the day. For scheduling: open Team schedule, tap Edit schedule, add employees to the roster, click a day cell, choose the shift, and save.

Step 5 of 7

If a person is missing, fix Employees.

Go to Admin > Employees, search their name, turn on On schedule, set Type to Storefront or Stream, then come back to Team schedule.

Step 6 of 7

Post updates where the team sees them.

Announcements admin is for schedule changes, policy reminders, and team notes. The dashboard shows the latest posts so people do not have to hunt for them.

Step 7 of 7

Ask for help from any page.

Use Ask for help whenever a schedule, queue, announcement, or employee workflow looks off. It sends the page context so the issue is easier to fix.
